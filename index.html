About My Art

At Kaur Art Studio, every piece I create tells a story. I specialize in mandalas, traditional Indian motifs, calligraphy, tattoo designs, and custom hand-painted designs. Each artwork is carefully drawn with fine detailing, vibrant colors, and a touch of personal expression.

My goal is to bring calm, beauty, and inspiration into every home, office, or personal space. Whether it’s a wall hanging, a canvas gift, or a custom order, each creation is made with passion and precision.

What I Draw

  • Mandala Art: Hand-drawn patterns that promote mindfulness and peace.
  • Traditional Designs: Indian cultural motifs, floral and geometric patterns.
  • Custom Portraits: Personalized portraits or illustrations on canvas.
  • Decorative Artwork: Unique designs for home décor, gifts, or special occasions.
  • Calligraphy: Beautifully styled letters and word art for décor or gifts.
  • Tattoo Designs: Custom sketches and tattoo-ready designs with creative flair.
